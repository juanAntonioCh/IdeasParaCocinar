Tortilla de patatas Submitted by Juan Antonio

Tortilla de Patatas, a classic Spanish dish, consists of thinly sliced potatoes and onions enveloped in a flavorful egg mixture. This savory delight is a staple in Spanish cuisine and a perfect choice for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 potatoes (peeled and thinly sliced)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 5-6 eggs
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced potatoes and cook until they are tender and slightly browned.
  3. Remove the potatoes and sauté the chopped onion in the same pan until it becomes translucent.
  4. Beat the eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the cooked potatoes and onions to the eggs and mix well.
  6. Heat a bit of olive oil in the pan, pour in the egg mixture, and cook until the edges are set.
  7. Flip the tortilla using a plate and cook until it's fully set and golden brown.
  8. Serve the tortilla de patatas warm or at room temperature.
