Gazpacho Submitted by Juan Antonio

Gazpacho, a refreshing Spanish cold soup, is crafted from a medley of fresh vegetables, including tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, and onions. Blended to a smooth consistency, it serves as a delightful chilled dish, perfect for warm summer days.

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 lime (juice and zest)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh basil or cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the ripe tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, and red onion.
  2. Minimize garlic cloves and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the chopped vegetables, minced garlic, tomato juice, red wine vinegar, and olive oil.
  4. Using a blender or food processor, blend the mixture until smooth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to let the flavors meld.
  7. Serve the gazpacho cold, garnished with fresh basil or cilantro.
