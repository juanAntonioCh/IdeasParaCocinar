Quinoa-white-chicken-chili Submitted by Juan Antonio

Quinoa is great because it is both high in protein and gluten free. It's a great grain to add to a delicious and healthy white chili.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pound boneless and skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1-2 cups of corn (optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice (~1/2 lime)
  • 1 avocado

Instructions

  1. Cook the onion and jalapeño in olive oil over medium heat until tender.
  2. Add the garlic and cumin and cook until fragrant, about a minute
  3. Add the broth, salsa verde, chicken, beans, quinoa and oregano
  4. Reduce the heat and simmer until the chicken is cooked and the quinoa is tender, about 10-15 minutes
  5. Garnish with sliced avocado cilantro
