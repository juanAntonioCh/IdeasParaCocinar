How to Peel a Tomato Submitted by Juan Antonio

Tomatoes, versatile in salads, rich in sauces, can be a juicy challenge to slice. Optimal results come from selecting a ripe yet firm tomato. If the tomato is overly ripe, it might be challenging to slice neatly but could be effortlessly scooped for pulp. Approach with care to avoid squishing the tender fruit while cutting.

Method

To cut a tomato, start by washing it thoroughly. Place the tomato on a cutting board and use a sharp knife to slice off the stem end. Then, cut the tomato in half vertically. For diced tomatoes, place each half flat on the cutting board and make horizontal and vertical cuts, adjusting the size according to your preference. To slice the tomato, lay each half flat and make even slices. Be cautious with your fingers and use a proper cutting technique to ensure safety.
